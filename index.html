Our Rules

Originality

- All Work must be orginal and their own.
- Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
- Properly cite any sources or referenced (if any) used in your projects or compositions.

English and Urdu Essay

- Write essays independently.
- Handwritten submissions are mandatory; typed work will not be accepted.
- Include a first draft to demonstrate your revision process.

Poster Design

- Conduct experiments individually; group projects are not allowed.
- Maintain a detailed logbook documenting your process, observations and results.
- Prepare a brief verbal presentation explaining your project and findings.

General Guielines

- Submit a signed declaration form a parent or gurdian confirming independent completion of the work.
- Adhere to all deadlines; late submissions will not be accepted.
- Be prepared to answer questions about your during assessment.
